What does Cyber Security mean?

The term cyber security can be used to phrase many different areas of protecting critical systems and sensitive information from digital attacks. This does not not only refer to attacks against digital information. It also goes over how digital attacks can be conducted on infrastructure systems such as power grids, health systems, banking systems and many other vital information that needs to be protected.

To combat against cyber attacks, it is important to have a strategy in place to protect your systems. This includes the protection over you and business that protect your stuff as well as others. The scope of the strategy can vary in topcis of this catigory.
